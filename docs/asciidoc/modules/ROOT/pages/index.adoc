== Why These Languages?

=== Spanish

* **Literature** — Reading Cervantes, García Márquez, Borges in original
* **Cultural bridge** — 500+ million speakers worldwide
* **Professional** — Serving Spanish-speaking communities
* **Personal** — The joy of another world of expression

=== Portuguese

* **Global reach** — 260+ million speakers
* **Business** — Brazil as major economic power
* **Literature** — Pessoa, Machado de Assis
* **Linguistic bridge** — Spanish accelerates Portuguese

== The Romance Language Advantage

Spanish and Portuguese share ~89% lexical similarity:

* **Shared vocabulary** — cognates accelerate acquisition
* **Similar grammar** — verb conjugation patterns transfer
* **Phonetic awareness** — distinguishing similar sounds sharpens listening
* **Cultural context** — Iberian and Latin American history intertwines

== Learning Methodology

[arabic]
. **Comprehensible input** — massive reading and listening
. **Spaced repetition** — vocabulary retention
. **Active production** — writing and speaking from day one
. **Cultural immersion** — media, music, native content
. **Systematic grammar** — structured study alongside input
